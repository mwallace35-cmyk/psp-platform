### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - Archbishop Carroll fells Episcopal Academy

December 15, 2025: Radnor, PA 19087

The [Archbishop Carroll](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=73286316-d429-4d2f-a2b9-4ce36ee447de) Patriots basketball team scored 77 points and limited the visiting [Episcopal Academy](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=67645ba2-15d5-4ee2-a1a5-6dbca9800a9d) Churchmen to 63 in the Patriots non-league triumph on Monday.

The Patriots now have a 2-2 record. They play next when they travel to [Sagemont Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6eebc20b-0d1f-4e87-81eb-adbd6302c936) for a non-league game on Friday, December 19. [Archbishop Carroll](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=73286316-d429-4d2f-a2b9-4ce36ee447de) will confront a Lions team coming off a [64-61](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=ce2f98e6-907e-4677-ab14-1adcdda92238) non-league win over [Sports Leadership & Management](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=2f58a19b-52f2-4eb2-acad-d133f520caa6) (Miami, FL). The Lions record now stands at 7-3.

The Churchmen (2-3) will now prepare for their outing against [Friends' Central](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=7f28c78a-c416-4c96-a70f-6da9b4943342) (Wynnewood, PA). The go into the non-league game with a 2-9 record.
