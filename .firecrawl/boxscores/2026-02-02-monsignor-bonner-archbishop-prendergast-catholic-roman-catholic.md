### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic bests Roman Catholic

February 2, 2026: Drexel Hill, PA 19026

The [Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cc43e649-2f69-4eb3-aa76-92e4f022cb68) Friars/Pandas basketball team scored 71 points and limited the visiting [Roman Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cfedd35b-8252-4b69-948f-3a2318cdce69) Cahillite to 59 in the Friars/Pandas league triumph on Monday.

The Friars/Pandas now possess a 15-5 record. They put it on the line next when they travel to [Cardinal O'Hara](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=b7cb2446-a770-4731-933c-9df0d2ecf964) for a [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) outing on Friday, February 6. [Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cc43e649-2f69-4eb3-aa76-92e4f022cb68) will clash with a Lions squad coming off a [52-49](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=c00465f4-6d6b-4b88-8681-5c98e3601466) league win over [Lansdale Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=5c8aaa0a-a9d3-4f12-b076-320ab46b8368) (Lansdale, PA). The Lions record now stands at 7-11.

The Cahillite (14-7) will now prepare for their outing against [Devon Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cc3da412-4e2c-414b-8830-fa239d2f8d8d) (Devon, PA). The Tide come into the [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) outing with a 5-15 record. In their last bout, [Devon Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cc3da412-4e2c-414b-8830-fa239d2f8d8d) achieved victory over [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac) (Philadelphia, PA), [53-44](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=4f751b6a-dba5-4678-ba63-90c4df36816a), in a league bout.
