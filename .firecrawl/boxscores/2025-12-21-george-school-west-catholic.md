### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - West Catholic triumphant over George School

December 21, 2025: Newtown, PA 18940

The crowd at Sunday's non-league challenge between the [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac) Burrs (Philadelphia, PA) and the host [George School](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=55e84249-b4aa-4fe9-9060-40b47e59677a) Cougars (Newtown, PA), observed [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac)'s basketball team overwhelm [George School](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=55e84249-b4aa-4fe9-9060-40b47e59677a) by a score of [63-27](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=1bbc3520-4ffe-4c1d-82e9-0c1afa459901).

The Burrs (2-2) will now prepare for their challenge against [Plymouth Whitemarsh](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=8c148c7d-58bf-47ed-92c1-c6d1154de8fb) (Plymouth Meeting, PA). The Colonials come into the non-league challenge with a 4-3 record. In their last challenge, [Plymouth Whitemarsh](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=8c148c7d-58bf-47ed-92c1-c6d1154de8fb) was crushed by [Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac) (Warminster, PA), [64-62](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=1e08e07e-31da-4b8f-b42a-dd3ed43e55aa), in a non-league battle.

The Cougars now own a 1-5 record. They put it on the line next when they travel to [Germantown Friends](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=38b0ef11-28f2-4519-a006-0dc3dca6b74a) for a [Friends Schools](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=fbf54816-f10b-414a-ab87-c570710f082e&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) battle on Tuesday, January 6. [George School](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=55e84249-b4aa-4fe9-9060-40b47e59677a) will meet a squad coming off a [49-43](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=fe91877b-26e0-432b-a296-a37e4346b0b2) non-league loss to [Girard College](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6e106885-313d-4376-98ea-50f98f0cf563) (Philadelphia, PA). The record now stands at 3-7.
