### New York High School Basketball - Holy Cross nudges Imhotep Charter

December 28, 2025: Flushing, NY 11358

The crowd at Sunday's non-league challenge between the [Holy Cross](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6228aa73-260a-4345-8456-b42028ae822b) Knights (Flushing, NY) and the visiting [Imhotep Charter](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=616abd7a-0e54-4529-b3cd-c75d50c5c88c) Panthers (Philadelphia, PA), observed [Holy Cross](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6228aa73-260a-4345-8456-b42028ae822b)'s basketball team edge [Imhotep Charter](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=616abd7a-0e54-4529-b3cd-c75d50c5c88c) by a score of [71-68](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=e6e2d786-ee3a-4a0b-8790-b0f1bddfa09a).

The Knights (5-9) will now prepare for their challenge against [St. Francis Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6c48af6d-9eb7-4755-b554-fef48f802a5e) (Fresh Meadows, NY). The Terriers enter the [Catholic AA - Brooklyn/Queens](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=1a387257-a3ca-4cf6-9811-a1e69e96b9fb&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) match with a 6-3 record. In their last match, [St. Francis Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6c48af6d-9eb7-4755-b554-fef48f802a5e) tipped [Iona Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=81839177-494d-4310-9e2f-ab2273b7a82e) (New Rochelle, NY), [63-45](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=5668bd0a-281c-4c9b-bd66-594ef58d1622), in a non-league contest.

In their next game, the Panthers battle the [West Philadelphia](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=edef4033-0c57-4461-82aa-e8f710920c5a) Speedboys (Philadelphia, PA) in a [Philadelphia Public - Independence](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=1ccbefde-968c-4b05-8439-1aace69ccf94&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) contest, on Friday, January 2. [Imhotep Charter](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=616abd7a-0e54-4529-b3cd-c75d50c5c88c) will attempt to better on its 6-4 season record.
