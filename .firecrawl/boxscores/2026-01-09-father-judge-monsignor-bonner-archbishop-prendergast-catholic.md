## Game Story

### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - Father Judge can't hold off Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic

January 9, 2026: Drexel Hill, PA 19026

[Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cc43e649-2f69-4eb3-aa76-92e4f022cb68) Friars/Pandas (Drexel Hill, PA) supporters left the game satisfied Friday as they witnessed their basketball squad nudge the visiting [Father Judge](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=8f547781-04a9-42c9-b2f2-ad8b8ec8507f) Crusaders (Philadelphia, PA), [64-63](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=1b110613-4b4a-4fa1-bd24-1b3291831e84) in a league match.

With the victory, [Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=cc43e649-2f69-4eb3-aa76-92e4f022cb68&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) advances its record to 9-2 on the year. The Friars/Pandas travel to [Salesianum](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=5376efd4-bd09-4bc0-b701-3022f8e986b5&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Wilmington, DE) to confront the Sallies in a non-league contest on Sunday, January 11. The Sallies go into the contest with a record of 5-6.

In their next game, the Crusaders confront the [Devon Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cc3da412-4e2c-414b-8830-fa239d2f8d8d) Tide (Devon, PA) in a [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) contest, on Monday, January 12. [Father Judge](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=8f547781-04a9-42c9-b2f2-ad8b8ec8507f) will attempt to better on its 4-8 season record. The Tide go into the contest with a 2-10 record after their [57-50](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=8517fef1-2304-42d6-b29e-faef364db77f) non-league loss to [Abraham Lincoln](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6c31430b-e3e8-4d94-9c08-d7e77401655a) (Philadelphia, PA).
