Pleasant, UT) supporters left the game satisfied Monday as they witnessed their basketball squad triumph over the host [Roman Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cfedd35b-8252-4b69-948f-3a2318cdce69) Cahillite (Philadelphia, PA), [74-41](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=6f89cc62-1064-49e7-b7fe-b54f426b0c07) in a non-league match.

With the victory, [Wasatch Academy](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=b7d00c42-1ff4-4d28-84ba-1d201f572153&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) advances its record to 9-4 on the year. The Tigers travel to [Layton Christian Academy](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=7bfd7955-5184-4b6c-9dfe-ef2d1c105ac6&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Layton, UT) to face the Eagles in a non-league match on Tuesday, January 13. The Eagles enter the match with a record of 13-1.

The Cahillite now own a 5-4 record. They play next when they travel to [Bishop McNamara](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=2259a5d8-90dd-4049-a3cf-1f7a4cea455b) for a non-league contest on Tuesday, December 30. [Roman Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cfedd35b-8252-4b69-948f-3a2318cdce69) will battle a Mustangs team coming off a [58-55](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=95b1c895-e659-44e3-bbae-eeb9a1fe6b31) non-league win over [St. Xavier](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6fd2176b-408c-4fa6-a097-094cdba695e5) (Louisville, KY). The Mustangs record now stands at 7-5.
