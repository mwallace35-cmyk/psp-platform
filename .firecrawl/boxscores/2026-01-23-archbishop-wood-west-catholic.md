January 23, 2026: Warminster, PA 18974

They put it on the line next when they host [Sidwell Friends](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=f03caf68-8c6c-4a2e-b0d6-85e949c78a27) for a non-league test on Saturday, January 24. The Quakers record now stands at 12-4.
