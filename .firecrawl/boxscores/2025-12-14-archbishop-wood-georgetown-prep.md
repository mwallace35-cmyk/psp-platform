### Maryland High School Basketball - Archbishop Wood nips Georgetown Prep

December 14, 2025: North Bethesda, MD 20852

The crowd at Sunday's non-league challenge between the [Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac) Vikings (Warminster, PA) and the host [Georgetown Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=b57a75d3-464b-44a5-9c4a-a2fd2f86cd9c) Little Hoyas (North Bethesda, MD), observed [Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac)'s basketball squad hold off [Georgetown Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=b57a75d3-464b-44a5-9c4a-a2fd2f86cd9c) by a count of [52-48](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=a0e85a08-8b88-411f-b785-7c2d1f3be6ce).

With the victory, [Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) advances its record to 2-3 on the year. The Vikings host [Plymouth Whitemarsh](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=8c148c7d-58bf-47ed-92c1-c6d1154de8fb&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Plymouth Meeting, PA) to battle the Colonials in a non-league contest on Saturday, December 20. The Colonials go into the contest with a record of 2-2.

In their next game, the Little Hoyas battle the [St. Mary's Ryken](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=fed168db-de2b-4f6b-9188-36aafec21768) Knights (Leonardtown, MD) in a non-league contest, on Saturday, December 20. [Georgetown Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=b57a75d3-464b-44a5-9c4a-a2fd2f86cd9c) will attempt to better on its 3-4 season record.
