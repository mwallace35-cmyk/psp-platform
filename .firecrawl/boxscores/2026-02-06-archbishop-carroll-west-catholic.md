### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - Archbishop Carroll nudges West Catholic

February 6, 2026: Radnor, PA 19087

The crowd at Friday's league challenge between the [Archbishop Carroll](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=73286316-d429-4d2f-a2b9-4ce36ee447de) Patriots (Radnor, PA) and the visiting [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac) Burrs (Philadelphia, PA), observed [Archbishop Carroll](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=73286316-d429-4d2f-a2b9-4ce36ee447de)'s basketball team edge [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac) by a score of [39-33](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=bc4ebb22-e883-4a6e-b529-a9bb3a757c77).

The Patriots (12-9) will now prepare for their challenge against [Cardinal O'Hara](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=b7cb2446-a770-4731-933c-9df0d2ecf964) (Springfield, PA). The Lions come into the [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) challenge with a 7-12 record. In their last challenge, [Cardinal O'Hara](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=b7cb2446-a770-4731-933c-9df0d2ecf964) was tipped by [Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=cc43e649-2f69-4eb3-aa76-92e4f022cb68) (Drexel Hill, PA), [62-50](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=1e142a2b-611a-4c93-b222-b873b91946aa), in a league battle.

The Burrs now own a 10-10 record. They put it on the line next when they travel to [Father Judge](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=8f547781-04a9-42c9-b2f2-ad8b8ec8507f) for a [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) battle on Sunday, February 8. [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac) will meet a Crusaders squad coming off a [60-49](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=596d8e1f-dec9-4697-83f4-1b96af3f9412) league win over [St. Joseph's Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=e6ce254d-240b-4081-a66e-26e1ced9a095) (Philadelphia, PA). The Crusaders record now stands at 11-10.
