The [Imhotep Charter](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=616abd7a-0e54-4529-b3cd-c75d50c5c88c&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Panthers (Philadelphia, PA) basketball crushed the visiting [Hill Freedman World Academy Hawks (Philadelphia, PA)](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=97dcbe0c-5b51-48bf-97a5-381100ad66a6&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c), [68-14](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=bcecf7be-1798-4135-b7ba-43a8da5851e8&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in Saturday's non-league bout.
