December 9, 2025: Hyattsville, MD 20781

The [DeMatha](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=f0d82e69-dabf-44e0-a131-32581fac97dc) Stags basketball team scored 74 points and limited the visiting [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac) Burrs to 47 in the Stags non-league triumph on Tuesday.
