### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - West Catholic knocks off Colonia

December 28, 2025: Philadelphia, PA 19139

The [Colonia](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=49051b03-55df-4799-971d-c15f6eadd7cd&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Patriots basketball squad (Colonia, NJ), lost [61-42](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=7f0bb753-c81c-45da-a869-026d7cef52b1&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in Sunday's non-league battle with the host [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Burrs (Philadelphia, PA)

The Burrs travel to [La Salle College](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=9c259289-a8cc-494d-aa7b-7c959ddef1d3&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Wyndmoor, PA) to meet the Explorers in a [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) battle on Friday, January 2. The Explorers enter the battle with a record of 5-3. [La Salle College](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=9c259289-a8cc-494d-aa7b-7c959ddef1d3&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) won [73-69](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=169b6a0d-aa67-4943-b205-ef3e3f4629f7&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in their recent non-league battle against [La Salle Academy](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=d8551162-8f20-4d89-b853-4d9759fe883f&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Providence, RI)

The Patriots now own a 2-5 record. They put it on the line next when they host [Piscataway](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=bc712fca-24ec-405f-9cb4-e71b5df15140) for a [Greater Middlesex - Red](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=b3724384-10b0-49cf-8b04-ec6fc9a4de8b&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) contest on Tuesday, January 6.
