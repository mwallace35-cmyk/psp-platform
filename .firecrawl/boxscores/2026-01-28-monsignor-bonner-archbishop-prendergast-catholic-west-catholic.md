## Game Story

### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - West Catholic falls to Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic

January 28, 2026: Drexel Hill, PA 19026

The [West Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=412e4766-53bb-4753-8afa-8153e778b6ac&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Burrs basketball squad (Philadelphia, PA), was outscored [71-56](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=1ff91ec0-cb94-4d6c-a069-b70c1d3c2cb2&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in Wednesday's league battle with the host [Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=cc43e649-2f69-4eb3-aa76-92e4f022cb68&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Friars/Pandas (Drexel Hill, PA)

With the victory, [Monsignor Bonner/Archbishop Prendergast Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=cc43e649-2f69-4eb3-aa76-92e4f022cb68&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) advances its record to 14-4 on the year. The Friars/Pandas travel to [Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Warminster, PA) to meet the Vikings in a [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) match on Friday, January 30. The Vikings enter the match with a record of 12-6. [Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) won [46-41](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=dab387ca-85fa-4da3-88f4-669d3f6bfe07&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in their recent league match against [Devon Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=cc3da412-4e2c-414b-8830-fa239d2f8d8d&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Devon, PA)

The Burrs now own a 9-8 record. They put it on the line next when they travel to [Archbishop Carroll](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=73286316-d429-4d2f-a2b9-4ce36ee447de) for a [Philadelphia Catholic](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=02ff0419-5a7b-4a31-bd91-b35c0950af44&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) match on Friday, February 6.
