In their next game, the encounter the [Hun](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=bb970e66-4734-444b-a636-bf736028859f) Raiders (Princeton, NJ) in a non-league challenge, on Tuesday, November 25.
