## Game Story

### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - Paul VI crushes Father Judge

November 29, 2025: Philadelphia, PA 19136

The [Paul VI](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=1c3ccc31-4ddf-40e7-8700-070f311148b4&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Panthers (Chantilly, VA) basketball crushed the host [Father Judge Crusaders (Philadelphia, PA)](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=8f547781-04a9-42c9-b2f2-ad8b8ec8507f&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c), [90-40](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=705aa9cb-ddc3-4d64-b475-efbff54166be&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in Saturday's non-league bout.

The Panthers now possess a 1-0 record. They play next when they host [Westfield](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=6ea75389-6bbc-449b-a930-d9daf8b75770) for a non-league bout on Thursday, December 4.

The Crusaders (0-1) will now prepare for their test against [Archbishop Spalding](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=fa67a40c-5261-46c2-9db1-631b71b38334) (Severn, MD). The Cavaliers come into the non-league test with a 2-1 record.
