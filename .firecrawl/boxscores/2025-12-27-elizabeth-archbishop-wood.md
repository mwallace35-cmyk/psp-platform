### Pennsylvania High School Basketball - Elizabeth toppled by Archbishop Wood

December 27, 2025: Warminster, PA 18974

[Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac) Vikings (Warminster, PA) supporters left the game satisfied Saturday as they witnessed their basketball squad notch a victory over the visiting [Elizabeth](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=1993fa24-70a9-467c-87cd-a19246401a7b) Minutemen (Elizabeth, NJ), [76-61](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=b93b606d-8e36-457b-96f8-c10380e20df9) in a non-league match.

With the victory, [Archbishop Wood](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=328f7292-bd41-4019-9d13-4490f088adac&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) betters its record to 4-4 on the campaign. The Vikings travel to [Archbishop Carroll](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=38dcb8ed-66d5-41ba-b062-ff5f77eba32e&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Washington, DC) to battle the Lions in a non-league contest on Sunday, December 28. The Lions go into the contest with a record of 8-3. [Archbishop Carroll](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=38dcb8ed-66d5-41ba-b062-ff5f77eba32e&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) won [79-69](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=9a32046f-7be6-45d4-b49a-b3a4668bf244&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in their recent non-league contest against [Oak Ridge](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=045008c9-a67e-410b-bbcd-8fde1af24c51&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Orlando, FL)

In their next game, the Minutemen battle the [Varina](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=b3b001d3-750e-4da4-8b60-ddaa2cbc8831) Blue Devils (Richmond, VA) in a non-league match, on Monday, December 29. [Elizabeth](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=1993fa24-70a9-467c-87cd-a19246401a7b) will attempt to upgrade on its 2-4 season record. The Blue Devils enter the match with a 0-2 record after their [55-49](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=e210de94-b2a6-4c7b-aeab-149fbfb99cc7) non-league loss to [Massaponax](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=54f4470d-cdea-45b4-a1bf-3d01090fed37) (Spotsylvania, VA).
