### Maryland High School Basketball - St. Andrew's Episcopal falls to St. Joseph's Prep

December 14, 2025: Potomac, MD 20854

The [St. Andrew's Episcopal](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=ad146642-eba0-4b95-b45f-aebb68d22f2a&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Lions basketball squad (Potomac, MD), was outscored [65-53](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?contestid=7a32bc12-7534-4ae8-a1c6-49998e5ff2c6&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) in Sunday's non-league battle with the visiting [St. Joseph's Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=e6ce254d-240b-4081-a66e-26e1ced9a095&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) Hawks (Philadelphia, PA)

With the victory, [St. Joseph's Prep](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=e6ce254d-240b-4081-a66e-26e1ced9a095&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) advances its record to 2-4 on the season. The Hawks host [Parkland](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?schoolid=e8a3b321-ebe6-46d8-9d56-6ca2088ce8e0&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) (Allentown, PA) to battle the Trojans in a non-league challenge on Sunday, December 21. The Trojans come into the challenge with a record of 4-1.

In their next game, the Lions battle the [Sidwell Friends](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=f03caf68-8c6c-4a2e-b0d6-85e949c78a27) Quakers (Washington, DC) in a [DCSAA AA](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/league/standings.aspx?leagueid=3e76668c-536f-4a5f-939e-b602cb039ee8&ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c) challenge, on Wednesday, December 17. [St. Andrew's Episcopal](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=ad146642-eba0-4b95-b45f-aebb68d22f2a) will attempt to better on its 3-3 season record. The Quakers come into the challenge with a 1-4 record after their [65-57](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/stats/boxscore.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&contestid=ce9e8bea-425a-4683-9e09-454911dc9a77) non-league loss to [Bishop O'Connell](https://www.maxpreps.com/local/team/schedule.aspx?ssid=ee7cf537-7394-4430-88ca-becaabda286c&schoolid=897e2baa-2f99-453e-bb33-d063aa488c2c) (Arlington, VA).
