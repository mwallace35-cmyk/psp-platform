Page-by-Page Redesign Guide

Tab-style navigation pills replace scattered stat counters. Aesthetically pleasing layouts that flow. Every page mapped to the war room report.

March 18, 2026

Design Direction

Mike: "I don't love the numbers that show players, schools, and titles all over the place. I would like to utilize that style for tabs maybe."

BEFORE: Big stat counter pills in hero: "36,218 players / 2,942 schools / 446 titles"

AFTER: That pill style becomes interactive TAB NAVIGATION. Numbers move into context or disappear.

Tab Pills Pattern

Overview Teams Leaderboards Records Championships Awards

Active: filled with sport color, white text. Inactive: outlined. Same pattern on every page.

Flow Principles

PrincipleHow
No orphaned numbersStats appear IN context (tables, cards) not as hero decorations
2-column layoutMain content (65%) + sidebar (35%) on desktop. Single on mobile.
Card-based sectionsEvery block is a card with border, radius, consistent padding
Sport color threadingActive tabs, accent borders, hovers all use the sport accent color

Hero Background — Field/Court Textures

Each sport gets a unique surface texture with 80-85% navy overlay. NO color gradients. Just enough texture to feel the sport's playing surface.

SportTexture
FootballGreen turf / grass fibers
BasketballHardwood court grain
BaseballRed clay / dirt infield
TrackRubber track surface with lane lines
LacrosseTurf field (shared with football)
WrestlingMat texture / rubber surface
SoccerGrass pitch / natural turf
